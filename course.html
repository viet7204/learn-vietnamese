Vietnamese Tones & Introduction

Master the 6 Vietnamese tones, learn basic greetings, and count from 0-10. Introduction to the Vietnamese alphabet and pronunciation system.

Greetings & Self-Introduction

Family & Relationships

Talk about family members, describe relationships, use possessive structures, and understand Vietnamese kinship terms and age hierarchy.

Numbers, Time & Calendar + Mid-term

Food & Dining

Order food at restaurants, express preferences, discuss Vietnamese cuisine, and learn dining etiquette. Practice menu reading and restaurant role-plays featuring iconic dishes like bún chả, bánh mì, and cà phê sữa đá.

120 minutes 50-60 words Beginner
Daily Conversations Listen & Practice Practice Exercises Lesson Plan (MD)

Shopping & Money

Negotiate prices at markets, describe items by color and size, count money, and practice bargaining. Learn about Vietnamese currency and shopping culture.

Directions & Transportation
