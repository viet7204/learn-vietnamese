Assessment Rubrics

Vietnamese for Adult Beginners Course

Oral Presentation Conversation Written Work Listening Participation Homework & Quizzes Mid-term Week 8 Grading Scale

1. Oral Presentation Rubric 20 points

Assessment: Week 12 Final Project

Pronunciation & Tones (6 points)

Level Points Description
Excellent 5-6 Tones are consistently accurate; pronunciation is clear and understandable; minimal errors that don't impede communication
Good 3-4 Tones are generally accurate with occasional errors; pronunciation is mostly clear; some errors but meaning is still conveyed
Developing 1-2 Frequent tone errors that sometimes impede understanding; pronunciation needs improvement; communicates basic ideas despite errors
Needs Work 0 Tone accuracy is lacking; pronunciation unclear; difficult to understand even with context

Vocabulary & Grammar (5 points)

Level Points Description
Excellent 4-5 Uses rich, varied vocabulary; grammatical structures are accurate and varied; minimal errors
Good 3 Uses adequate vocabulary; basic grammatical structures are used correctly; some errors that don't impede communication
Developing 1-2 Limited vocabulary; simple structures with frequent errors; meaning is conveyed with difficulty
Needs Work 0 Very limited vocabulary; many grammatical errors; unable to convey meaning effectively

Fluency & Confidence (4 points)

Level Points Description
Excellent 4 Speaks smoothly with natural pauses; confident delivery; good eye contact; minimal reliance on notes
Good 3 Generally fluent with some hesitation; reasonably confident; some reliance on notes
Developing 2 Frequent pauses and hesitation; appears nervous; heavy reliance on notes
Needs Work 0-1 Very halting delivery; lacks confidence; reads entirely from notes

Content & Organization (3 points) + Time Management (2 points)

  • Content (3 pts): Well-organized with all required elements = 3 | Organized, most elements = 2 | Some missing = 1 | Many missing = 0
  • Time (2 pts): 4-6 minutes = 2 | 3-4 or 6-7 minutes = 1 | Under 3 or over 7 = 0

2. Conversation Assessment Rubric 20 points

Assessment: Week 12

Comprehension (6 points)

Level Points Description
Excellent 5-6 Understands all questions without repetition; responds appropriately; can follow topic changes
Good 3-4 Understands most questions; occasionally needs repetition; generally follows conversation
Developing 1-2 Understanding is limited; frequently needs repetition; struggles to follow conversation
Needs Work 0 Little to no comprehension; cannot respond appropriately even with repetition

Response Quality (6 points) + Pronunciation (4 points) + Interactive Communication (4 points)

Similar detailed rubrics apply for each category. See full guide for details.

3. Written Work Rubric 15 points

Assessment: Week 8 & 12 Portfolio

  • Content & Completeness (5 pts): All elements + rich detail = 4-5 | All elements, adequate = 3 | Some missing = 1-2 | Many missing = 0
  • Vocabulary & Grammar (5 pts): Varied vocabulary, correct grammar = 4-5 | Adequate, mostly correct = 3 | Limited, frequent errors = 1-2 | Very limited, many errors = 0
  • Tones & Orthography (3 pts): Consistently correct = 3 | Mostly correct = 2 | Frequent errors = 1 | Many errors = 0
  • Organization (2 pts): Well-organized = 2 | Somewhat = 1 | Disorganized = 0

4. Listening Comprehension Rubric 15 points

Assessment: Week 12

Question Responses (12 points)

  • Multiple choice (6 questions): 6 points
  • Short answer (4 questions): 4 points
  • True/False (2 questions): 2 points

Following Directions (3 points)

  • 3 pts: Answered all questions, followed all instructions
  • 2 pts: Answered most, minor errors
  • 1 pt: Missing several answers
  • 0 pts: Many unanswered, didn't follow instructions

5. Class Participation Rubric 20 points

Assessment: Ongoing Throughout Course

Participation Breakdown

Attendance 5 pts
Active Participation 8 pts
Preparation & Homework 5 pts
Attitude & Effort 2 pts

Attendance Scoring

  • 5 pts: Perfect or near-perfect (0-1 absences)
  • 4 pts: Very good (2 absences)
  • 3 pts: Good (3 absences)
  • 2 pts: Moderate (4-5 absences)
  • 0-1 pts: Poor (6+ absences)

Active Participation Levels

Excellent 7-8 Consistently volunteers; actively participates in all activities; engages with pairs/groups; takes risks speaking Vietnamese
Good 5-6 Frequently participates; engages in most activities; contributes to pair/group work
Developing 3-4 Sometimes participates when called on; minimal voluntary contributions; adequate group work
Needs Work 0-2 Rarely participates; passive in activities; does not contribute to pair/group work

6. Homework & Weekly Quiz Rubric 25 points

Assessment: Ongoing

Weekly Quiz Performance (15 points total)

Average of all weekly quizzes (typically 10-12 quizzes)

  • Convert quiz scores to percentage
  • Percentage × 15 = points earned
  • Example: Average quiz score 85% → 0.85 × 15 = 12.75 points

Homework Completion & Quality (10 points total)

Excellent 2 Complete, thorough, on time; demonstrates effort and understanding
Good 1.5 Complete, on time; adequate effort
Developing 1 Incomplete or late; minimal effort
Needs Work 0-0.5 Missing, very late, or insufficient effort

Calculate: Average homework score × 5 = final homework points

7. Mid-term Progress Check 15 points

Assessment: Week 4

Mid-term Components

Oral Interview (Introduction, Greetings, Q&A, Tones) 8 pts
Written Vocabulary Test (25 items) 5 pts
Listening Exercise (4 questions) 2 pts

8. Week 8 Progress Evaluation 15 points

Assessment: Week 8

Conversation Simulation (6 points)

Scenario: Order food, ask for directions, describe daily routine

  • Task Completion (2 pts): Completes all three scenarios
  • Communication (2 pts): Conveys meaning effectively
  • Pronunciation (2 pts): Tones and pronunciation are understandable

Reading Comprehension (4 points)

Short passage (8-10 sentences) with 4 comprehension questions @ 1 point each

Writing Task (5 points)

Task: Describe your daily routine (8-10 sentences)

  • Vocabulary (2 pts) + Grammar (2 pts) + Tones (1 pt)

Overall Course Grading

Total Possible Points: 100

Class Participation & Engagement 20 pts
Weekly Homework & Quizzes 25 pts
Mid-term Assessment (Week 4) 15 pts
Progress Evaluation (Week 8) 15 pts
Final Project & Assessment (Week 12) 25 pts
