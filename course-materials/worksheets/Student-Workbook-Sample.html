Course Syllabus Overview

Course Goals

By the end of 12 weeks, you will be able to:

  • Pronounce Vietnamese tones accurately
  • Introduce yourself and others
  • Handle common daily situations (ordering food, shopping, directions)
  • Have basic conversations on familiar topics
  • Understand slow, clear speech about everyday matters
  • Read simple texts with familiar vocabulary
  • Write basic messages and descriptions
  • Demonstrate cultural awareness

Weekly Time Commitment

  • Class Time: 2 hours per week
  • Homework: 1-2 hours per week (10-20 minutes daily is ideal)
  • Total: 3-4 hours per week

Practice Tip:

Listen to native speakers and repeat! Use the audio resources provided in the course. Practice each tone slowly and deliberately before trying to speak quickly.

Practice Exercises

Exercise 1: Listen to your teacher and circle the tone you hear

Exercise 2: Practice saying each tone 10 times

Exercise 3: Record yourself and compare to native speaker audio
