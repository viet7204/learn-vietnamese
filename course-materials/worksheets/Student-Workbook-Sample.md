### Vocabulary (20-30 words)

**Core Greetings:**

**Numbers 0-10:**
□ Không (0), Một (1), Hai (2), Ba (3), Bốn (4), Năm (5)  
□ Sáu (6), Bảy (7), Tám (8), Chín (9), Mười (10)  

### The Six Vietnamese Tones

Vietnamese is a **tonal language** - the pitch/melody of your voice changes the meaning of words!

#### Tone Chart with "ma"

| # | Tone Name | Mark | Syllable | Meaning | Pitch Pattern |
|---|-----------|------|----------|---------|---------------|
| 1 | Mid-level | (none) | ma | ghost | → (flat) |
| 2 | High rising | á | má | mother | ↗ (go up) |
| 3 | Low falling | à | mà | but | ↘ (go down) |
| 4 | Rising | ả | mả | tomb | ↗↘ (curve) |
| 5 | Creaky rising | ã | mã | horse | ↘↗ (dip & rise) |
| 6 | Heavy falling | ạ | mạ | rice seedling | ↓ (drop low) |

**Hand Gestures for Tones:**

3. ________ (low falling: hand drops down)

### Cultural Note: Vietnamese Alphabet

Vietnamese uses the Latin alphabet (like English!), making it easier for English speakers to read than Chinese or Thai.

The alphabet was introduced by French missionaries in the 17th century and is called **"chữ quốc ngữ"** (national language script).

Special letters in Vietnamese:
- Ă, Â, Đ, Ê, Ô, Ơ, Ư (plus tone marks)

### Cultural Note: Vietnamese Names

Vietnamese names are written: **FAMILY NAME + MIDDLE NAME + GIVEN NAME**

Example: **Nguyễn Văn An**
- Nguyễn = family name (like "Smith")
- Văn = middle name
- An = given name (what people call you)

Most common family names: Nguyễn, Trần, Lê, Phạm, Hoàng

In class, you can use your English name, or choose a Vietnamese name if you like!

## QUICK REFERENCE: COMMON PHRASES

### Survival Vietnamese

**Greetings:**
- Xin chào - Hello
- Tạm biệt - Goodbye
- Hẹn gặp lại - See you again

**Politeness:**
- Cảm ơn - Thank you
- Cảm ơn nhiều - Thank you very much
- Không có gì - You're welcome
- Xin lỗi - Sorry/Excuse me

**Basic Responses:**
- Vâng/Dạ - Yes (polite)
- Không - No
- Được - OK/Can do
- Không sao - It's okay/No problem

**Useful Phrases:**
- Tôi không hiểu - I don't understand
- Bạn có thể nói lại không? - Can you repeat?
- Nói chậm hơn, được không? - Speak slower, please?
- … tiếng Anh là gì? - What is... in English?
- Cái này là gì? - What is this?

**Emergency:**
- Giúp tôi! - Help me!
- Bệnh viện ở đâu? - Where is the hospital?
- Tôi bị lạc - I'm lost
