# WEEK 6: Shopping & Money

### Learning Objectives
- Negotiate prices at markets
- Ask "How much?" and understand prices
- Count money (Vietnamese đồng)
- Describe clothing items and colors
- Express sizes and preferences
- Practice basic haggling

### Vocabulary (50-60 words)
**Shopping Actions:**
Mua (buy), Bán (sell), Tiền (money), Đồng (currency), Bao nhiêu? (how much?), Đắt (expensive), Rẻ (cheap), Giảm giá (discount)

**Colors:**
Đỏ (red), Xanh lá cây (green), Xanh dương (blue), Vàng (yellow), Trắng (white), Đen (black), Nâu (brown), Hồng (pink)

**Sizes:**
Nhỏ (small), Vừa (medium), Lớn (large)

**Items:**
Áo (shirt), Quần (pants), Giày (shoes), Túi (bag), Sách (book), Mũ (hat)

### Lesson Structure (120 min)

#### Warm-up (15 min)
- Review last week: Order food role-play
- Number review 0-100 (preparation for prices)

#### Presentation (20 min)
- Vietnamese currency (đồng)
- Common price ranges
- Market vs store shopping culture
- Bargaining etiquette

**Key Phrases:**
- Cái này bao nhiêu tiền? (How much is this?)
- Đắt quá! (Too expensive!)
- Rẻ hơn được không? (Can you make it cheaper?)
- Được, tôi mua. (OK, I'll buy it)

#### Pronunciation Practice (15 min)
- Color vocabulary drills
- Large numbers practice
- Tone practice with prices

#### Guided Practice (25 min)

**Activity 1: Price Tag Reading**
- Read Vietnamese prices from images
- Convert numbers to Vietnamese words

**Activity 2: Color Matching Game**
- Match color words to objects
- Describe clothing colors

**Activity 3: "This One or That One?"**
- Practice: Cái này (this one), Cái kia (that one)
- Pair practice pointing and asking

#### Communicative Practice (30 min)

**Activity 1: Market Simulation**
- Set up "market" in classroom
- Students rotate: buyer/seller roles
- Practice bargaining dialogues

**Dialogue Template:**
```
Buyer: Cái này bao nhiêu tiền?
Seller: Một trăm nghìn đồng.
Buyer: Đắt quá! Chín mươi nghìn được không?
Seller: Được. (OK) / Không được. (No)
```

**Activity 2: Shopping List Challenge**
- Give students budget and shopping list
- Visit different "vendors" in class
- Find best prices
- Report back on purchases

#### Application Activity (10 min)
**"Best Deal" Presentations**
- Students share their shopping strategies
- Vote on best bargainer

#### Wrap-up & Homework (5 min)

**Homework:**
1. Create illustrated vocabulary poster with colors
2. Practice counting money (use play money or real)
3. Write shopping dialogue (10 lines)
4. Research: Vietnamese markets vs American stores (comparison essay)
5. Vocabulary flashcards for Week 6

### Assessment
- Bargaining role-play completion
- Price comprehension check
- Color identification quiz
- Participation in market simulation

# WEEK 7: Directions & Transportation

### Learning Objectives
- Ask for and give directions
- Name transportation methods
- Understand cardinal directions
- Express distance and duration
- Navigate using basic maps
- Take taxi or motorbike taxi

### Vocabulary (50-60 words)
**Directions:**
Trái (left), Phải (right), Thẳng (straight), Rẽ (turn), Gần (near), Xa (far)

**Transportation:**
Xe buýt (bus), Taxi (taxi), Xe máy (motorbike), Xe đạp (bicycle), Đi bộ (walk), Bay (fly)

**Places:**
Khách sạn (hotel), Bệnh viện (hospital), Ngân hàng (bank), Bưu điện (post office), Sân bay (airport), Nhà hàng (restaurant)

**Questions:**
Ở đâu? (where?), Như thế nào? (how?), Bao xa? (how far?), Bao lâu? (how long?)

### Lesson Structure (120 min)

#### Warm-up (15 min)
- Review shopping vocabulary with quick quiz
- Show images of Vietnamese streets/traffic

#### Presentation (20 min)
- Basic direction vocabulary
- Transportation options in Vietnam
- Vietnamese addresses format
- Safety tips for travelers

**Direction Phrases:**
- Đi thẳng (go straight)
- Rẽ trái (turn left)
- Rẽ phải (turn right)
- Ở phía trước (in front)
- Ở phía sau (behind)

#### Pronunciation Practice (15 min)
- Direction commands drill
- Transportation vocabulary
- Place names practice

#### Guided Practice (25 min)

**Activity 1: Simon Says - Vietnamese Style**
- Teacher gives direction commands
- Students follow: đi thẳng, rẽ trái, rẽ phải, dừng lại (stop)

**Activity 2: Map Reading**
- Simple map of Vietnamese district
- Identify landmarks
- Practice: "[Place] ở đâu?" (Where is [place]?)

**Activity 3: Transportation Survey**
- Students ask: "Bạn đi làm bằng gì?" (How do you go to work?)
- Record responses
- Graph results

#### Communicative Practice (30 min)

**Activity 1: Direction Giving Practice**
- Pairs: one has map, one doesn't
- Give directions to specific locations
- Switch roles

**Activity 2: Lost Tourist Simulation**
- Some students are "lost tourists"
- Others are "helpful locals"
- Tourists ask for directions to landmarks
- Locals give directions in Vietnamese

**Sample Dialogue:**
```
Tourist: Xin lỗi, khách sạn ở đâu?
Local: Đi thẳng, rồi rẽ trái. Khách sạn ở bên phải.
Tourist: Cảm ơn!
```

**Activity 3: Taking a Taxi**
- Practice phrases:
  - Đến [place] (To [place])
  - Bao nhiêu tiền? (How much?)
  - Ở đây (Here/Stop here)

#### Application Activity (10 min)
**"Navigate the City" Team Game**
- Teams race to navigate map
- Follow Vietnamese directions
- First to reach destination wins

#### Wrap-up & Homework (5 min)

**Homework:**
1. Draw map of your neighborhood with Vietnamese labels
2. Write directions from your home to 3 places
3. Watch: Hanoi traffic video (reaction essay)
4. Learn: Vietnamese address format
5. Practice: Direction vocabulary with flashcards

### Assessment
- Direction-giving accuracy
- Map reading comprehension
- Transportation vocabulary quiz
- Role-play participation

# WEEK 8: Daily Routines & Activities

### Learning Objectives
- Describe daily schedule
- Express habitual actions
- Talk about hobbies
- Use frequency adverbs
- Discuss weekly routines
- Make plans with others

### Vocabulary (60-70 words)
**Daily Activities:**
Thức dậy (wake up), Ngủ (sleep), Làm việc (work), Học (study), Ăn sáng (breakfast), Ăn trưa (lunch), Ăn tối (dinner), Nghỉ (rest), Tập thể dục (exercise), Đọc sách (read), Xem TV (watch TV)

**Time Expressions:**
Buổi sáng (morning), Buổi trưa (noon), Buổi chiều (afternoon), Buổi tối (evening), Ban đêm (night)

**Frequency:**
Luôn luôn (always), Thường (usually), Đôi khi (sometimes), Hiếm khi (rarely), Không bao giờ (never), Mỗi ngày (every day)

### Lesson Structure (120 min)

#### Warm-up (15 min)
- Share direction maps from homework
- Quick review game: transportation charades

#### Presentation (20 min)
- Daily routine vocabulary introduction
- Vietnamese work/life culture
- Typical Vietnamese daily schedule
- Frequency adverb usage

**Model Daily Routine:**
```
Tôi thức dậy lúc sáu giờ sáng.
(I wake up at 6 AM)

Tôi ăn sáng lúc bảy giờ.
(I eat breakfast at 7:00)

Tôi đi làm lúc tám giờ.
(I go to work at 8:00)
```

#### Pronunciation Practice (15 min)
- Activity vocabulary drills
- Frequency adverb practice
- Sentence rhythm practice

#### Guided Practice (25 min)

**Activity 1: Daily Schedule Chart**
- Create personal daily schedule
- Label activities in Vietnamese
- Include times

**Activity 2: Frequency Practice**
- Sort activities by frequency
- Practice sentences: "Tôi [frequency] [activity]"
- Example: Tôi thường đọc sách (I usually read books)

**Activity 3: "What Time Do You...?"**
- Pair interviews about daily routines
- Record partner's schedule
- Present to another pair

#### Communicative Practice (30 min)

**Activity 1: "A Day in the Life"**
- Students present typical day
- Use visual timeline
- Class asks questions

**Activity 2: "Weekly Routine Comparison"**
- Compare weekday vs weekend routines
- Find similarities/differences with partner

**Activity 3: "Making Plans"**
- Practice invitations:
  - Ngày mai bạn làm gì? (What are you doing tomorrow?)
  - Bạn muốn đi [activity] không? (Do you want to go [activity]?)
- Accept/decline invitations politely

**Dialogue:**
```
A: Thứ Bảy bạn có rảnh không? (Are you free Saturday?)
B: Rảnh. Sao? (Free. Why?)
A: Chúng ta đi xem phim nhé! (Let's go see a movie!)
B: Được! Mấy giờ? (OK! What time?)
```

#### Application Activity (10 min)
**"Perfect Day" Presentations**
- Describe ideal daily schedule
- No work, all fun activities!
- Vote on most interesting day

#### Wrap-up & Homework (5 min)

**IMPORTANT: Week 8 Progress Evaluation Next Week!**

**Homework:**
1. Write detailed daily routine essay (15-20 sentences)
2. Create weekly schedule poster with Vietnamese labels
3. Record video describing your typical day (2-3 min)
4. Interview family member about their routine (in English, present in Vietnamese)
5. **Study for Week 8 Evaluation:**
   - Review Weeks 5-8 vocabulary
   - Practice conversation scenarios
   - Prepare to describe daily routine

### Week 8 Progress Evaluation (Next Class - 15 points)

**1. Conversation Simulation (6 points):**
- Order food at restaurant
- Ask for directions
- Describe daily routine

**2. Reading Comprehension (4 points):**
- Read short passage about someone's day
- Answer 4 comprehension questions

**3. Writing Task (5 points):**
- Write 8-10 sentences describing daily routine
- Use time expressions
- Use frequency adverbs

### Assessment
- Daily routine presentation
- Vocabulary usage in writing
- Conversation simulation performance
- Participation in activities

# WEEK 9: Health & Body Parts

### Learning Objectives
- Name major body parts
- Describe health problems
- Visit doctor or pharmacy
- Express pain and symptoms
- Ask for help in medical situations
- Understand basic health advice

### Vocabulary (50-60 words)
**Body Parts:**
Đầu (head), Mắt (eyes), Tai (ears), Mũi (nose), Miệng (mouth), Răng (teeth), Tay (hand/arm), Chân (leg/foot), Bụng (stomach), Lưng (back), Tim (heart)

**Health:**
Đau (pain/hurt), Ốm (sick), Khỏe (healthy), Bệnh viện (hospital), Bác sĩ (doctor), Y tá (nurse), Thuốc (medicine), Nhà thuốc (pharmacy)

**Symptoms:**
Sốt (fever), Ho (cough), Đau đầu (headache), Đau bụng (stomachache), Cảm lạnh (cold), Mệt (tired)

### Lesson Structure (120 min)

**Warm-up (15 min):** Return Week 8 evaluations, discuss progress

**Presentation (20 min):** Body parts introduction with visuals, health vocabulary, medical situations in Vietnam

**Pronunciation Practice (15 min):** Body part drills, symptom vocabulary, medical phrases

**Guided Practice (25 min):**
- Activity 1: Body parts labeling diagram
- Activity 2: Symptom matching (symptom to body part)
- Activity 3: "Tôi bị đau..." (I have pain in...) practice

**Communicative Practice (30 min):**
- Activity 1: Doctor role-play (doctor/patient pairs)
- Activity 2: Pharmacy visit simulation
- Activity 3: Emergency phone call practice

**Sample Dialogue:**
```
Patient: Xin chào, tôi bị đau đầu.
Doctor: Bạn ốm mấy ngày rồi?
Patient: Ba ngày rồi.
Doctor: Bạn cần uống thuốc và nghỉ ngơi.
```

**Application Activity (10 min):** Health advice presentations

**Wrap-up & Homework (5 min):** Body parts diagram for homework, health vocabulary flashcards

# WEEK 10: Weather & Seasons

### Learning Objectives
- Describe weather conditions
- Talk about seasons
- Discuss temperature
- Make weather-appropriate plans
- Compare climates
- Understand Vietnam's tropical climate

### Vocabulary (40-50 words)
**Weather:**
Thời tiết (weather), Trời (sky/weather), Nóng (hot), Lạnh (cold), Mưa (rain), Nắng (sunny), Gió (wind), Mây (cloudy)

**Seasons:**
Mùa xuân (spring), Mùa hè (summer), Mùa thu (fall), Mùa đông (winter), Mùa mưa (rainy season), Mùa khô (dry season)

**Expressions:**
Hôm nay trời thế nào? (How's the weather?), Trời nóng quá! (It's so hot!), Trời đang mưa (It's raining)

### Lesson Structure (120 min)

**Warm-up (15 min):** Health vocabulary review game

**Presentation (20 min):** Vietnam's climate regions, seasons in Vietnam vs other countries, weather vocabulary with images

**Pronunciation Practice (15 min):** Weather vocabulary drills, season names, descriptive phrases

**Guided Practice (25 min):**
- Activity 1: Weather forecast reading
- Activity 2: Season preference survey
- Activity 3: Climate comparison (Vietnam vs student countries)

**Communicative Practice (30 min):**
- Activity 1: Weather report presentations
- Activity 2: Planning activities based on weather
- Activity 3: "What to wear?" discussions

**Sample Conversation:**
```
A: Hôm nay trời thế nào?
B: Hôm nay trời nóng và nắng.
A: Chúng ta đi biển nhé!
B: Tốt quá!
```

**Application Activity (10 min):** Create weather forecast for Vietnam

**Wrap-up & Homework (5 min):** Keep weather diary for one week

# WEEK 11: Hobbies & Interests

### Learning Objectives
- Talk about hobbies and pastimes
- Express preferences
- Make and respond to invitations
- Discuss free time activities
- Sports and entertainment vocabulary
- Plan social activities

### Vocabulary (50-60 words)
**Hobbies:**
Sở thích (hobby), Đọc sách (reading), Xem phim (watching movies), Nghe nhạc (listening to music), Du lịch (travel), Nấu ăn (cooking), Chụp ảnh (photography)

**Sports:**
Bóng đá (soccer), Bơi (swimming), Chạy bộ (running), Cầu lông (badminton), Thể thao (sports)

**Preferences:**
Thích (like), Yêu (love), Ghét (hate), Thích nhất (favorite)

**Invitations:**
Đi...không? (Want to go...?), Được (OK), Xin lỗi, tôi bận (Sorry, I'm busy)

### Lesson Structure (120 min)

**Warm-up (15 min):** Weather diary sharing, climate discussion

**Presentation (20 min):** Hobby vocabulary, Vietnamese leisure activities, free time culture

**Pronunciation Practice (15 min):** Hobby vocabulary drills, invitation phrases

**Guided Practice (25 min):**
- Activity 1: Hobby survey and graphing
- Activity 2: "Tôi thích..." sentence building
- Activity 3: Invitation dialogue practice

**Communicative Practice (30 min):**
- Activity 1: "Find someone who..." (hobby edition)
- Activity 2: Making weekend plans
- Activity 3: Hobby presentations (2 minutes each)

**Invitation Dialogue:**
```
A: Thứ Bảy bạn muốn đi xem phim không?
B: Xin lỗi, tôi bận. Chủ Nhật được không?
A: Được! Mấy giờ?
B: Hai giờ chiều.
```

**Application Activity (10 min):** "Introduce Your Hobby" presentations

**Wrap-up & Homework (5 min):**
- Prepare final project (due Week 12!)
- Study ALL vocabulary (Weeks 1-11)
- Practice presentation skills

# WEEK 12: Review & Final Project

### Learning Objectives
- Demonstrate comprehensive Vietnamese ability
- Integrate all learned content
- Present final project confidently
- Reflect on learning journey
- Plan for continued learning

### Week 12 Structure (120 min)

#### Part 1: Comprehensive Review (40 min)

**Review Games:**
1. **Vietnamese Jeopardy** (15 min)
   - Categories: Tones, Family, Food, Directions, Daily Life
   - Teams compete
   - Review all 11 weeks

2. **Pronunciation Olympics** (10 min)
   - Difficult tone combinations
   - Speed rounds
   - Individual challenges

3. **Conversation Speed Dating** (15 min)
   - Rotate partners every 2 minutes
   - Random conversation topics
   - Use all vocabulary learned

#### Part 2: Final Project Presentations (50 min)

**Final Project Requirements:**
5-minute presentation in Vietnamese including:
- Personal introduction (name, origin)
- Family description
- Daily routine
- Hobbies and interests
- Vietnamese food experience
- Why you learned Vietnamese
- Future plans for Vietnamese

**Presentation Format:**
- Can use visual aids (photos, posters, slides)
- Must be mostly in Vietnamese (can explain complex ideas in English)
- Q&A from classmates (in Vietnamese!)

**Assessment Rubric (25 points total):**
- Pronunciation & Tones: 6 points
- Vocabulary & Grammar: 5 points
- Fluency & Confidence: 4 points
- Content & Organization: 3 points
- Visual Aids & Creativity: 3 points
- Time Management: 2 points
- Q&A Handling: 2 points

#### Part 3: Final Written Assessment (20 min)

**Listening Comprehension (15 points):**
- 12 questions from audio clips
- Topics from all 12 weeks

**Reading Comprehension (Not timed - take home):**
- Read passage about Vietnamese person's life
- Answer comprehension questions

#### Part 4: Celebration & Closure (10 min)

**Activities:**
- Certificates of completion
- Class photo
- Vietnamese snacks (if available)
- Share: Most memorable moment
- Continued learning discussion
- Alumni group formation (optional)

**Course Evaluation:**
- Anonymous survey
- Feedback for improvement
- Testimonials (optional)

### Post-Course Resources

**Continued Learning:**
1. Intermediate Vietnamese course info
2. Language exchange partner connections
3. Online resources list
4. Vietnamese cultural events calendar
5. Recommended apps and websites

**Stay Connected:**
- Class Facebook/WhatsApp group
- Monthly meet-ups (optional)
- Vietnamese restaurant outings
